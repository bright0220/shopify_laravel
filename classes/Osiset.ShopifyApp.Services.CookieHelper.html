setCookiePolicy()

setCookiePolicy() : void

Sets the cookie policy.

From Chrome 80+ there is a new requirement that the SameSite cookie flag be set to none and the cookies be marked with secure.

Reference: https://www.chromium.org/updates/same-site/incompatible-clients

Enables SameSite none and Secure cookies on:

  • Chrome v67+
  • Safari on OSX 10.14+
  • iOS 13+
  • UCBrowser 12.13+
